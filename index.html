About me

My name is Paula and I am a junior web developer originally from Romania, currently residing in Italy. I have a passion for creating visually stunning and user-friendly websites. My approach to web development is to not only create a website that looks great, but also provides an enjoyable user experience.

I take pride in my strong work ethic and ambition, I am always willing to go the extra mile to achieve my goals.

With a strong background in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and ReactJS I am dedicated to crafting responsive designs that adapt to all devices and browsers. My portfolio showcases a selection of my work, including interactive web applications and responsive websites that demonstrate my skills and abilities as a developer. I also have a strong understanding of SQL from my studies and practice with building and manipulating databases. I am currently taking a Java and Spring Boot course to better understand object-oriented programming and to develop more programming skills.This will enable me to expand my skill set and take on more complex projects in the future.
I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to expand my skills, so please feel free to contact me for any upcoming projects or collaborations.
